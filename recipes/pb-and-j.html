Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Peanut Butter Jelly Time!

This recipe is a simple yet timeless childhood favorite of many.

Ingredients

  • Two slices bread per sandwich desired(preparer's choice of bread variety)
  • Peanut Butter 2 tablespoons per sandwich
  • Strawberry Preserves 2 tablespoons per sandwich
  • Butter 2 tablesoons per sandwich

Steps

  1. Spread 2 tablespoons of Penut Butter onto one slice of bread
  2. Spread 2 tablespoons of Strawberry Preserves onto another slice of bread
  3. Stack slices Strawberry Preserves side facing Peanut Butter side
  4. Spread 1 table spoon of butter on each side of the stacked sandwich
  5. Warm a 12-inch skillet to medium heat.
  6. Toast each side of sandwich in skillet until golden brown
  7. Remove sandwich, from heat, slice enjoy and repeat all previous steps per sandwich
